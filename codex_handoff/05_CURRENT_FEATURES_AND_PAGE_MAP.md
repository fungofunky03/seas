- Show a stylized job record.
- Drive to waitlist or explanation.

## Problem section

Purpose:

- Make the pain concrete.
- Name the operational gaps before talking about software.

## Done-for-you automation story

Purpose:

- Show the pain being automated behind the scenes.
- Contrast complex workflow design with simple owner POV.
- Reinforce “done-for-you automation” without turning SEAS into a generic agency.

Animated phases:

- Lead captured
- Scope built
- Quote prepared
- Crew handoff

## Replace-stack section

Purpose:
